Registration

Registration opens on Friday September 14, 2018, at 5:00 PM ET

  • Each attending school must be a registered member of their state Science Olympiad organization and have paid all applicable membership fees prior to tournament day.
  • Duke will only be holding a Division C tournament for 2019.
  • Any school can register up to 3 teams in the tournament until we are at capacity. We are looking to take 30 teams this year and teams registering after we are full will be placed on a waitlist.
  • There is a registration fee of $100, with $50 for each additional team.

Transportation

Parking (Arriving by Car/Bus)

For transportation by official team vehicles, parking will be provided. There will be an attendant to help direct and official vehicles can drop off in Science Drive parking lot. More information on visitor parking can be found here. We are working on obtaining a parking space for the invitational and will update this site when we receive confirmation.

Arriving by Train

The nearest Amtrak station to Duke University is (DNC) in Durham, NC. The station is located in the City Center District, where you can then take the Bull City Connector (BCC) to Duke University. The BCC is a fare-free route that runs every 17 minutes.
