Transportation

Parking (Arriving by Car/Bus)

Parking space and drop off/pick up locations can be found on a map here.

For transportation by official team vehicles, parking will be provided. There will be an attendant to help direct and official vehicles can drop off in Science Drive parking lot. More information on visitor parking can be found here

Arriving by Train

The nearest Amtrak station to Duke University is (DNC) in Durham, NC. The station is located in the City Center District, where you can then take the Bull City Connector (BCC) to Duke University. The BCC is a fare-free route that runs every 17 minutes.

Our Team

We are an undergraduate organization at Duke that is dedicated to improving and expanding the experience of high school students in Science Olympiad. Our activities include volunteering at local Science Olympiad competitions and organizing a high school invitational. 2019 will be our first year holding the tournament!
