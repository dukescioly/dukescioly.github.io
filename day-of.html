Competition Policies


We will run all National events, along with Picture This as a trial event.

  1. All tests/labs will be revised by our executive board for consistency, quality and appropriate difficulty spread, and all events will be run by Science Olympiad alumni or volunteering Duke students.
  2. All tests and keys with raw score statistics will be made available to teams after competition.
  3. Each team must be accompanied by at least one unique adult. Schools with two teams must thus be accompanied by at least two unique adults.
  4. Every team will be assigned a homeroom to stay in during competition day.
  5. Events will be scored according to the guidelines at https://www.soinc.org/scoring-guidelines.
  6. We will adhere to the following safety protocols with regards to eye protection and laser usage.
  7. Competitors must adhere to the code of ethics and policies outlined at soinc.org/join/policies.

Registration

Registration opens on Friday September 14, 2018, at 5:00 PM ET

  • Each attending school must be a registered member of their state Science Olympiad organization and have paid all applicable membership fees prior to tournament day.
  • Duke will only be holding a Division C tournament for 2019.
  • Any school can register up to 3 teams in the tournament until we are at capacity. We are looking to take 30 teams this year and teams registering after we are full will be placed on a waitlist.
  • There is a registration fee of $100, with $50 for each additional team.
